## How to read a recipe card

1. **Ingredients** tell you when to use it.
2. **Mental recipe** tells you the motion.
3. **Invariant / taste test** tells you why the state is valid.
4. **Core template** gives you the skeleton.
5. **Common mistakes** become review material in [[Mistake Pantry]].
