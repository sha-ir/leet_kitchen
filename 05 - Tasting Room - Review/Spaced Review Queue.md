# Spaced Review Queue

After each solved problem, schedule reviews by recognition strength.

| Review | When | What to do |
|---|---|---|

| Leftovers | Next day | Re-solve or trace from memory. |
| Second taste | 3 days | Identify pattern from prompt only. |
| Menu mix | 7 days | Compare with a confusable recipe. |
| Chef test | 21 days | Solve a disguised variant. |
